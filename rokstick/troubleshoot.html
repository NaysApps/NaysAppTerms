How to Connect Rok+ Remote to Your Roku TV

Follow these steps to connect your Rok+ Remote app to your Roku TV.

Step 1: Open the Rok+ Remote app on your mobile device.
Step 2: Ensure your Roku TV and the mobile device are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. The Rok+ Remote requires both devices to be on the same network to communicate.
Step 3: The app will try to automatically detect Roku devices on your network. Follow the on-screen instructions to select your Roku TV.

Troubleshooting

If you're having trouble connecting, here are some steps to help.

Device Not Found

Check your network settings to ensure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network. If they are on different networks, they will not be able to communicate with each other.

Try restarting your Roku TV by turning it off, waiting a few seconds, and then turning it back on. This can resolve connection issues.

Weak Wi-Fi Signal

Ensure your Roku TV and mobile device have a strong Wi-Fi signal. If the Wi-Fi signal is weak, try moving closer to your router or eliminating signal obstructions.

Updating the Rok+ Remote App

Make sure you are using the latest version of the Rok+ Remote app. Check the app store on your mobile device for any available updates and install them.

Restarting Your Mobile Device

If you continue to experience issues, try restarting your mobile device. This can resolve unseen issues affecting the app's performance.

Reinstalling the Rok+ Remote App

If none of the above steps work, try uninstalling and then reinstalling the Rok+ Remote app. This can fix issues caused by corrupt app data.
