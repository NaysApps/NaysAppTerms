How to Connect Rokstick Remote to Your Roku TV

Follow these steps to connect your Rokstick Remote app to your Roku TV.

Step 1: Open the Rokstick Remote app on your mobile device.
Step 2: Ensure your Roku TV and the mobile device are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. The Rokstick Remote requires both devices to be on the same network to communicate.
Step 3: The app will try to automatically detect Roku devices on your network. Follow the on-screen instructions to select your Roku TV.
Step 4: If prompted, enter the confirmation code displayed on your Roku TV into the Rokstick Remote app to confirm the connection.

Troubleshooting

If you're having trouble connecting, here are some steps to help.

Device Not Found

Check your network settings to ensure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network. If they are on different networks, they will not be able to communicate with each other.

Try restarting your Roku TV by turning it off, waiting a few seconds, and then turning it back on. This can resolve connection issues.
